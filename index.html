About Me

I am Amir-Hossein, a Computer Science PhD student at University of Maryland. I work with Perception and Robotic Group under supervision of Prof. Yiannis Aloimonos and Dr. Cornelia Fermüller.
My research focus is at the intersection of Computer Vision, Reinforcement Learning and Robotics which includes (1) perception for robotics, (2) state estimation and control, (3) sim2real transfer.
